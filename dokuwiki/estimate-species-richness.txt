====== Estimate species richness at given pH ======

**Dataset:** [[recol:data:vltava|Vltava river valley measurements]]

**Aim**: Estimate the species richness of forest vegetation at pH = 4.5.

**What to do:**:
  - Import the ''vltava.env'' data into R (you can do it directly using the script for [[recol:data:vltava#direct_import_of_data_into_r|Direct import of data into R]]).
  - Calculate linear regression between species richness and measured soil pH (variables ''SPEC.NO'' and ''pH'' in the dataset ''vltava.env'').
  - Predict the estimated species richness at pH = 4.5.
