====== What was the maximum measured temperature? ======

**Dataset:** //Temperature measurements// in [[recol:data|Example datasets]] section. The dataset represents time-series of temperature measurements done in 2007 in one locality within the Czech Republic. Three temperature loggers were used: S (150 cm above ground), M (5 cm below ground) and L (20 cm below ground).

**Aim:** What was the maximum temperature measured by each sensor (i.e. sensor S, M and L)?

**What to do:**
  - Import temperature data into R directly from the ''temperature.txt'' file on the website (no clipboard copy allowed).
  - Calculate **maximum** temperature for each of variables ''S'', ''M'' and ''L''
